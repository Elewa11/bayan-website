For Ministries of Education

A System-Wide Solution

Low literacy is a challenge facing the entire educational system, difficult to address with scattered solutions or inconsistent content. Bayan offers a scalable, systematic solution aligned with national framework stages, linking literacy to identity, civilization, knowledge, and Islamic reference—with aggregated dashboards and unified metrics to measure literacy on a large scale.

For Teachers

Lifts the Prep Burden off You

Arabic teachers spend significant time preparing materials, level-grading them, and grading assessments. Bayan relieves you of this burden: ready-made scientifically grounded content, clear lesson structuring from text to activities, automated grading for post-tests, and flexible assignment and tracking tools—so teachers can dedicate themselves to what they do best: teaching and guiding comprehension.

For Students

A Journey Tailored to Your Level

Students get frustrated when faced with a text above their level, bored when it is below, and detached from reading when it does not touch their world. Bayan offers you a reading journey precisely at your level, with rich content connecting you to your identity, civilization, and cognitive world, while pairing each topic with an inspiring role model makes learning feel closer to a story.
