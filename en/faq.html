Questions from Our Partners

Concise answers to the most frequently asked questions about Bayan. Can't find your question? Contact us.

What is the difference between Bayan and other Arabic learning platforms?›

Bayan focuses specifically on literacy rather than general language acquisition: its primary goal is to build comprehension, analysis, and critical thinking. It delivers this through content connecting reading to identity, civilization, and knowledge across four axes, and six carefully graded levels from kindergarten to university.

For whom was the platform designed?›

For private schools, ministries of education, teachers, and students from kindergarten to university, with accounts and dashboards customized for each role's specific needs.

How do you guarantee the scientific accuracy of the content?›

Every piece of content undergoes specialized academic review before publication. Furthermore, sacred and classical heritage texts are preserved exactly as written in their sources without modifications.

Does Bayan replace the school's core curriculum?›

No; it was designed to integrate and align with existing curricula rather than crowd them. Teachers can employ it flexibly as a supplementary resource within their lesson planning.

In what language is the content written?›

The educational content is written in Modern Standard Arabic (Fusha). The marketing and informational website interface is bilingual (Arabic & English).

How do we get started?›

By requesting a demo from our contact page. Our team will get in touch to assess your entity's needs and prepare a customized trial experience.
