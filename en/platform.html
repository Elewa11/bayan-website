Platform & Features

An Integrated Digital Environment

Bayan does not just deliver content; it integrates it within an environment serving every role: learners read and interact, teachers assign and track, while school administrations and ministries monitor performance at scale.

Role-Based Accounts

Dedicated interfaces and permissions for every role

RoleKey actions inside the platform
StudentEnters the reading library at their level, reads and interacts with vocabulary and analysis, performs activities and tests, and tracks their progress across levels.
TeacherSelects and assigns texts, grades essay answers and leaves feedback, views their class performance dashboard, and aligns content with their curriculum.
School AdminMonitors class and teacher performance, tracks progress against department goals, and manages accounts and subscriptions.
Ministry / EntityViews aggregated dashboards across schools, and measures Arabic literacy using unified national standards.
