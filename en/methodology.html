Methodology

How We Build the Thinking Reader

Six reading levels, two content tracks, and a ten-step lesson structure—an educational engineering that transitions the learner from text to understanding.

Levels

Six Reading Levels

Progression is not limited to text length, but encompasses depth of ideas, vocabulary density, sentence complexity, and the level of diacritization (tashkeel).

I
Kindergarten Narrative · Full Tashkeel
II
Early Elementary Narrative · Full Tashkeel
III
Elementary Narrative · Full Tashkeel
IV
Middle School Expository · Basic Tashkeel
V
High School Expository · Partial Tashkeel
VI
University Argumentative · Reduced Tashkeel

Note: Quranic texts remain fully diacritized exactly as written in their original source, without any reduction in any level.

Tracks

Two Complementary Content Tracks

TRACK 01

Generative Track

Pedagogically authored content that addresses a single thematic topic across all six levels under a unified cognitive framework. The text's depth, vocabulary, and style are tailored to fit the learner's developmental stage.

TRACK 02

Authentic Track

Documented original source texts (Quran, Hadith, classical poetry, prose, and tales) studied in a single-level lesson chosen by experts, preserving the text exactly as written in its source without modifications.
